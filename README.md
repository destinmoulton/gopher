### What is gopher?
***
gopher is a simple pub/sub message broadcast system.

### Usage
***
gopher("topic-name").subscribe(callback-function)
gopher("topic-name").broadcast(message-or-payload)
gopher("topic-name").unsubscribe(name-of-callback-function)

### Requirements
***
None.
